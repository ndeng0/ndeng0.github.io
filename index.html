Stony Brook Game Developers

Stony Brook Game Developers is a club dedicated to teaching others about game development and design. Every meeting a presenter, either an eboard member, student, or guest speaker, gives a presentation on an aspect on game development or the industry. Everything is discussed from how to build your first game to getting it out into the market. There are also the occasional game jams to see who builds the best game.

I have been a member of the club for almost 2 years and is currently an active memeber. Recently I have given a presentation on making games with Processing and I plan on doing more presentations in the future.

Stony Brook Esports

Stony Brook Esports is a club for those that are interested in competitive game or just playing games in general. Many of Stony Brook Esports teams are formed under the Esports club. Teams compete in tournaments against other universities in order to earn scholarships.

I have been a member of the club for almost 1 year and is currently an active member.

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical first person shooter with an emphasis on environmental destruction and teamwork.

The reason I like this game is due to its emphasis on teamwork. In order to win you must outsmart your opponents and manipulate the environment to your advantage. Being able to win by outsmart your opponents and teamwork is very satisfying. The game also keeps itself fresh by constantly putting out updates that changes the dymanics of the game.

Terraria

Terraria is a 2D sandbox game that takes place in a world filled with monsters and adventure.

The reason I like this game is due to its sandbox nature. You explore the world gathering resources in order to become better equiped against the monsters inhabiting the world. As you progress you'll encounter bosses that when defeated will drop valuable loot. Being able to build as you please and becoming stronger as time progress is very enjoyable for me.

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a first person shooter battle royale game recently released on PC.

The reason I like this game due to its unpredictable matches. When spawning in you have to pick up items and build up your arsenal against other players. Items you pick up are random and can be either good or bad. Losing while being unprepared can be frustrating, which make winning all the more satisfying.
